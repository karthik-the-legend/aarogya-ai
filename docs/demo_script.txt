STEP 1 — SET THE PROBLEM (60 seconds)
--------------------------------------
SAY: "This is Aarogya AI — a voice-first health assistant for
the 700 million rural Indians who can't access a doctor and
speak Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, or Kannada.

The problem with existing AI health tools is hallucination.
A plain language model recommends ibuprofen for dengue fever —
a drug that causes fatal internal bleeding in dengue patients.
My system grounds every response in verified WHO, CDC, and NIH
documents. Let me show you."

ACTION: Open huggingface.co/spaces/karthik55555/aarogya-ai
        Select "Hindi" in language dropdown

================================================================

STEP 2 — HINDI TEXT QUERY + SOURCES (90 seconds)
-------------------------------------------------
SAY: "A farmer in a Hindi-speaking village types this question."

ACTION: Paste Query 1 from demo_queries.txt:
        मुझे बुखार है और सिरदर्द है, क्या करूं?

SAY (while waiting): "The system translates to English,
searches the FAISS index across 87 medical document chunks,
and generates a grounded response using Llama 3.1."

AFTER RESPONSE APPEARS:
SAY: "Notice the green triage badge — safe to manage at home.
And here are the sources."

ACTION: Click the Sources expander
        Point to WHO/NIH PDF filenames

================================================================

--------------------------------------------
SAY: "Now let me show the safety layer — the most important part."

        सीने में दर्द है और सांस नहीं आ रही

Watch what happens."
